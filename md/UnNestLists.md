### Lists

Markdown supports ordered (numbered) and unordered (bulleted) lists.

*   Red
*   Green
*   Blue

Nested lists two levels

-   Red
	- Orange
	- Purple
-   Green
-   Blue
	- Dark Blue
	- Light Blue

Nested Lists with three levels

*   Red Level 1
	*    Red Level 2
	*    Orange Level 2
		*    Red Level 3
		*    Blue Level 3
*   Green
*   Blue
